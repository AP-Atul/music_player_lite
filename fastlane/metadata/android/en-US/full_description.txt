<i>Music Player Lite</i> is an elegant and light weight music player for Android that is highly motivated by <a href='https://github.com/enricocid/Music-Player-GO' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>Music Player GO</a>. Features include a.o.

* Listen to music laughing
* Manage the queue and shuffle/ repeat mode
* Change colors of the app sunglasses
* Manage folders to exclude(hide)
* Browse/ Sort/ Search albums and artists
* Change between Dark and Light Modes
* Create playlists by long pressing on music
